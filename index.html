About Me [CV]

Hi! My name is Keyang Xuan and currently I am a graduate student of University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I am working with Prof. Jiaxuan You. Before UIUC, I got my BS degree in Computer Science & Data Science at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, where I worked closely with Prof. Vipin Kumar and Prof. Kangjie Lu. My current research focus revolves around developing AI agents capable of engaging in socially intelligent, adaptive and trustworthy interaction.
